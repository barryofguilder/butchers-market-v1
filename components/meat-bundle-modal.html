Re-Packaging:

    Items are packaged as listed. There is an additional 10% charge for re-packaging or re-processing these items. For the courtesy of other customers please call in advanced notice for re-packaging requests.

    Substitutions:

    We allow limited substitutions for items within a package, and will be for the dollar value of that item, not the weight. If you want several items substituted, please choose the Mix & Match Packs instead.

    Availability:

    Occasionally, an item may be unavailable due to supply or time of day, such as nearing closing time.
